ORIGIN POINT: THE SILENCE

There was a time when singing stopped. Life circumstances. Fear. The weight of judgment. The voice that was once free became trapped behind walls of overthinking and self-doubt.

DISCOVERY: THE SONG

Then one day, a favorite song played. The body remembered before the mind did. Feet started moving. Voice started humming. Something ancient and true awakened. The body had never forgotten how to sing.

REVELATION: THE METHOD

If movement could bypass the analytical mind, what else could? Characters. Play. Embodiment. The realization: singing isn't a skill to be mastered through technique. It's a natural expression to be unlocked through freedom.

BREAKTHROUGH: THE NEUROSCIENCE

Research revealed specialized neurons that respond ONLY to singing. These pathways remain intact even when trauma silences the voice. The science confirmed what the body already knew: we're wired to sing.

MISSION: THE SPACESHIP

January 20th, 2025. The Sonic Spaceship launches. A workshop to help others rediscover their voices through movement, character, and play. Because if one person can find their voice again, so can everyone.

THE TRUTH ABOUT TIME

Time is not linear when it comes to healing.

You can lose your voice for years and rediscover it in a moment. You can struggle with something for months and breakthrough in seconds. You can carry wounds for decades and release them with a single song.

The Timeline isn't about how long it takes.
It's about the moment you decide to begin.
